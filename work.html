THE DAILY SNIPPET


The Daily Snippet was a not-for-profit entity, launched and operated by a group of volunteers in the Agnew School. They were offering a daily email service packed full of quirky and interesting facts to make you feel that extra bit more intelligent every day. We created a fun & interactive website to embrace the fun of learning. Our aim was to gain attention, engage & hook up on a daily dose of knowledge.
